Welcome

Welcome to my academic e-portfolio! This space documents my journey through the MSc Artificial Intelligence programme at the University of Essex Online.

Purpose of This Portfolio

This e-portfolio serves multiple purposes:

  1. Documentation – A comprehensive record of coursework, projects, and learning milestones
  2. Reflection – Critical analysis of my strengths, challenges, and growth throughout the programme
  3. Showcase – Demonstration of technical skills, creativity, and professional development
  4. Connection – Linking academic learning to broader social, ethical, and global contexts

What You’ll Find Here

Module Artefacts

Each module will have dedicated sections containing:

  • Coursework and assignments
  • Project documentation
  • Code repositories and technical implementations
  • Reflections on learning outcomes

Research & Publications

As I progress through the programme, I’ll share:

  • Research papers and literature reviews
  • Technical explorations and experiments
  • Findings from applied AI projects

Professional Development

This section tracks my growth in:

  • Employability skills
  • Communication and presentation abilities
  • Teamwork and collaboration
  • Leadership and project management

Reflections

Regular reflective entries will explore:

  • Challenges encountered and overcome
  • Key learnings and insights
  • Applications to real-world problems
  • Connections between theory and practice

About My Background

I bring 15+ years of software engineering experience from finance, banking, energy, and security sectors. This practical foundation enriches my academic study of AI, allowing me to bridge theoretical concepts with real-world applications.

My interests span:

  • AI & Machine Learning – particularly workflow mining and specification-driven development
  • Structured Workflows – applying modular, systematic approaches to complex problems
  • Responsible AI – ethical considerations and governance in AI deployment
  • Applied Research – translating academic insights into practical solutions

Looking Ahead

This portfolio will evolve throughout my MSc journey. I’m excited to explore cutting-edge AI topics, contribute to research, and develop solutions that address real-world challenges.

Thank you for visiting, and I hope you find this portfolio both informative and engaging!

Feel free to connect with me through the contact section or reach out via social media. I’m always interested in discussing AI, machine learning, and collaborative opportunities.
